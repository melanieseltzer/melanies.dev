`toolkit` is a monorepo that houses all of my tools and configs in one place. It attempts to lessen the hassle of setting up projects from scratch every time.

So far it contains all my linting rules as extensible shared configs, but I do intend for the scope to be broader and not limited to just lint/prettier configs.
